About Us

The department of Ceramic and Cement Technology was established in the year 1981 with a vision to provide learned and skilled engineers and to provide an exciting learning environment in the field of ceramic and cement technology to serve the local and global industries and institutes. Since its inception department has created more than 500 graduates who are serving in premier industries, to name a few BHEL, corning glass, JSW, ACC, Ultratech, Shree Cements, Saint-Gobin, Johnson, Bharat potteries, etc. !!
